Get your idea off the ground

Create a digital product like a movie, app, game, music or ebook – without the need for a project budget. 

Work with talented people

Programmers, musicians, writers, designers – connect with talented people to join commercial projects.

Manage your project

Track progress, monitor time, share files and communicate – you don't feel "remote" on Qravity.

Earn a fair share of profits

Music, films, audiobooks, games – whatever is your passion, you can follow it on Qravity! Every project created here is distributed through our marketplace. You will be receiving royalty income over the product life cycle.
